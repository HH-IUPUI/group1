Joseph

Joseph is an Information Science student at the University of Indiana, Bloomington. He enjoys bicycling, coding, and annoying cats (albeit unintentionally). In addition to a short stint as a Systems Administrator Joseph has been a teacher and (briefly) an international traveler to South Korea. If questioned persistently enough he might reveal a passion for Science Fiction and Fantasy literature and an absolutely abysmal command of the French language.

Maeve

Maeve is a native of Northwest Indiana that was recently transplated to West Central Indiana. She is working on her Masters in Library and Information at Indiana University Purdue University Indianapolis. She is a mother to two young humans and the wife of a wonderfully supportive husband. She is also a Children's Librarian at an award winning library. In her spare time she enjoys reading, music, nature, and learning. She is also an avid daydreamer where she travels all over the wrold.
