Amaru

My name is Amaru. I am 24 years old, born and raised in Chile. I moved to Finland in 2020 to serve in the Finnish army. After completing my military service, I started my studies in Software engineering at Haaga-Helia in January 2021. My first experience with programming languages was at 13 years old; I started to learn C and java but ended up training to build a network and playing with my friends in LAN that we created ourselves. I have always been exposed to a multicultural environment since I grew up with a Finnish mother and a Chilean father. Having moved to Finland from the other side of the world, I know what it’s like to feel homesick and experience a culture shock. Therefore, let’s discover how we can overcome these obstacles and grow stronger together!

Joseph

Joseph is an Information Science student at the University of Indiana, Bloomington. He enjoys bicycling, coding, and annoying cats (albeit unintentionally). In addition to a short stint as a Systems Administrator Joseph has been a teacher and (briefly) an international traveler to South Korea. If questioned persistently enough he might reveal a passion for Science Fiction and Fantasy literature and an absolutely abysmal command of the French language.

Maeve

Maeve is a native of Northwest Indiana, USA who was recently transplanted t West Central Indiana. She is a mother to two adorable young humans and a wife to a wonderfully supportive husband. She is currently working towards a Masters of Library and Information Science at Indiana University Indianapolis while managing a children's library. She often daydreams about traveling and fluently speaking foreign languages. Reading, nature, the arts, and learning are just a few of the things she enjoys.
